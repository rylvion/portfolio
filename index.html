About Me

Hi, I’m Abdurahman Sharif, a Level 3 Computer Science and Digital Development student at Manchester College. I’m passionate about coding, scripting, and game development, especially in Roblox Studio. I run my own wiki and website project called UnConventional, inspired by the webtoon unOrdinary and a Roblox game of the same name. I enjoy building interactive tools, learning new programming skills, and exploring creative tech projects.

I have experience with Python, Lua, HTML/CSS, and JavaScript, and I love experimenting with new frameworks and game mechanics. My goal is to create engaging games and digital experiences that combine creativity with coding. Outside of tech, I enjoy exploring web development trends, contributing to online communities, and collaborating on projects that challenge my skills.

Projects

Unconventional Abilities Visualizer

A web-based interactive stats visualizer for the Roblox game Unconventional. Users can compare different abilities and see their power, agility, defense, intelligence, and perception in a radar chart.

  • Technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Chart.js, Python
  • Features: Interactive radar chart, responsive design, user-friendly interface
  • Live Demo: View Project
  • Data Source: Google Sheets

Unconventional Wiki

An unofficial wiki for the Roblox game Unconventional, created and maintained solely by me as the admin. The wiki provides detailed information on game mechanics, items, and strategies. Currently, it contains 15 pages, with a goal of expanding to 100 pages.

  • Technologies: Fandom wiki platform, MediaWiki markup
  • Features: Comprehensive game guides, user contributions, regularly updated content
  • Note: ⚠️ This wiki is unofficial and is not affiliated with or endorsed by the developers of Unconventional. This wiki is also incomplete for the time being
  • Link: Visit Wiki
